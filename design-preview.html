📖 POI Catalog — one responsive field (Czech, Karoo OS look)

Category is an uppercase coloured label, actions are Karoo pills (‹ ghost left, › blue-grey right, matching the hardware buttons). Eat/drink cards headline live hours ("Otevřeno do 20:00") instead of a fake story. Tap anywhere → detail.

📄 POI detail (Activity · Open Sans · type-aware)

A real full-screen page in the Karoo OS language: 53 px top bar with the category as title, hairline dividers, uppercase grey row labels with dark bold values, pills bottom-left (Zpět) and bottom-right (Další ›) aligned to the physical buttons. SIGHT reads story-first; EAT/DRINK is practical-first with a big live-hours verdict and no fake story. Every row hides when its data is absent.
